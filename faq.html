Follow these steps to place an order:

  1. Visit the product page for an item you wish to buy.
  2. Click Add to Cart.
  3. When you've finished adding items to your cart, click on the Shopping Cart icon.
  4. If you need to edit the items in your Cart, click on Save for later or Delete next to the item in the Cart.
  5. Tap Proceed to checkout and follow the instructions.
  6. Review your order information and tap Place your order to complete your purchase.
